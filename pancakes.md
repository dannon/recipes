Grandma Baker's Pancake Recipe
==============================

-	2c flour
-	2c milk
-	2 eggs
-	5 tsp baking powder
-	1 tsp salt
-	1/3 cup canola oil
-	1 tsp vanilla
-	Cinnamon to taste
-	2-3 tbsp white sugar

Mix everything together well, pour pancakes.
When bubbles start forming in middle, flip.  Don't burn them.
